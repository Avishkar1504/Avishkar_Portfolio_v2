07. INTRODUCING "COMFORT RIDES"

We developed an innovative app for BMTC aimed at enhancing the convenience of public transportation for commuters. This user-friendly application provides real-time tracking of buses, route planning, and fare estimation, ensuring a seamless travel experience. With features like notifications for arrivals and delays, users can navigate the city with confidence. By streamlining the journey and improving accessibility, this app empowers passengers to make informed travel decisions, ultimately transforming their commuting experience.

Banashankari Bus Stand

The purpose of this study is to understand the problems faced by the citizens, while commuting from the BANASHANKARI BUS STAND to their specific destination. It is also, to identify an opportunity of design intervention to solve problems.

Problems Observed

The bus stand grapples with unhygienic conditions, paper ticket litter, dirty washrooms, pickpocketing, bus lane confusion, and etc. Urgent attention to these issues is vital for improving functionality, safety, and overall user experience.

About App

“Comfort Rides” is an app we developed for BMTC to streamline bus travel across the city. Designed for ease and convenience, the app allows users to check real-time bus timings, ensuring they stay informed about arrival and departure schedules. With its user-friendly interface, Comfort Rides enhances the overall commuting experience, helping passengers plan their trips more efficiently and enjoy a smoother journey on BMTC buses.
